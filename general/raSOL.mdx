## üí∞ Why LSTs Trade Above SOL

Unlike SOL, which is a static token, LSTs like raSOL **accumulate rewards directly into their value**:

- Each raSOL represents **1 SOL + staking rewards** earned since it was minted.
- Over time, the **exchange rate increases** (e.g., 1 raSOL = 1.03 SOL).
- When you redeem raSOL, you always get back **more SOL than you originally deposited**, depending on accrued rewards.

<Note>
  This is why LSTs often appear **‚Äúmore expensive‚Äù** than SOL on price charts.\
