# Multiply

Multiply is Hubra's higher-conviction Earn experience for people who want their assets to do more, without juggling a pile of tabs, protocols, and manual steps.

Instead of leaving assets idle, Multiply surfaces supported loop opportunities inside Hubra and packages them into a cleaner, easier flow.

<Info>
  Multiply is designed to feel simple on the surface while still giving you visibility into what asset is being used, what loop exposure you are taking, and the headline APY on offer.
</Info>

---

## What Multiply does

Multiply helps you:

- put supported assets to work from inside Hubra
- access loop-based yield opportunities without stitching the flow together yourself
- compare opportunities by asset, APY, and leverage profile
- stay in control while Hubra handles the ugly parts of the UX

In the app, Multiply opportunities are presented as cards with:

- the supplied asset
- the loop asset or exposure
- the current headline APY
- the maximum leverage available
- the protocol provider backing the opportunity

---

## Supported opportunity types

Based on the current Multiply integration, Hubra surfaces loop opportunities for selected asset groups, including:

- **Stablecoins** like USDC-style assets
- **SOL and liquid staking assets** such as hubSOL and raSOL
- **BTC-linked assets** such as cbBTC and xBTC
- **Selected basket-style assets** like JLP

The exact list can evolve over time as integrations improve and new markets meet Hubra's quality bar.

## How it feels in practice

The point of Multiply is not to make DeFi look more advanced.

It is to make it feel more usable.

That means:

1. **You choose an opportunity** instead of assembling one manually
2. **You review the headline terms** like APY and max leverage
3. **You open the deposit flow in Hubra**
4. **Hubra handles the interaction path** so you are not bouncing across multiple products just to get one position live

This is the same product philosophy behind the rest of Hubra: less operational overhead, more clear intent.

---

## What you should pay attention to

Multiply is meant to reduce friction, not hide risk.

Before opening a position, pay attention to:

- **Asset exposure** - what you are supplying and what loop you are taking
- **APY** - yields change and are not guaranteed
- **Maximum leverage** - the upper limit is not a recommendation
- **Protocol provider** - opportunities depend on third-party DeFi infrastructure

<Tip>
  "Up to" APY and "up to" leverage are product labels, not promises. Treat them as range indicators, then size positions accordingly.
</Tip>

---

## Risk notes

Multiply can increase complexity under the hood even when the interface feels clean.

Important risks include:

- **Smart contract risk** in underlying protocols and integrations
- **Market risk** from changes in collateral values and loop economics
- **Liquidation risk** when leverage is involved
- **Variable yield risk** because rates can move quickly

<Warning>
  Multiply is not the same as a passive savings account. Higher potential upside usually comes with more sensitivity to market conditions and leverage.
</Warning>
