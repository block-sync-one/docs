# Instant Unstake

Hubra gives you two ways to unstake your SOL: **Instant** for immediate liquidity, or **Slow** for maximum returns. You can also unstake partial amounts.

## Instant unstake

Convert your staked SOL to liquid SOL immediately. Powered by [Sanctum](https://sanctum.so).

**How it works:**
1. Select your stake account
2. Choose "Instant" mode
3. See the output amount and fees
4. Confirm the transaction
5. SOL arrives in your wallet within seconds

<Info>
Hubra covers all network fees. You only pay the Sanctum liquidity fee.
</Info>

### Fees

| Fee | Amount | Who pays |
|-----|--------|----------|
| Sanctum fee | 0.1-0.5% | You |
| Network fee | ~0.000005 SOL | Hubra (gasless) |

The exact Sanctum fee depends on available liquidity. You'll see the fee before confirming.

<Warning>
If price impact exceeds 5%, you'll see a warning. Consider using Slow mode for large amounts.
</Warning>

## Slow unstake

Standard Solana unstaking. Your SOL goes through the native cooldown period.

**How it works:**
1. Select your stake account
2. Choose "Slow" mode
3. Confirm the deactivation
4. Wait 2-3 days (1 full epoch)
5. Withdraw your full SOL amount

### Fees

| Fee | Amount | Who pays |
|-----|--------|----------|
| Sanctum fee | None | - |
| Network fee | ~0.000005 SOL | Hubra (gasless) |

<Tip>
The app shows how much more SOL you get with Slow mode. Use this to decide if waiting is worth it.
</Tip>

## Partial unstake

Unstake any amount - you don't have to unstake everything at once.

**Example:** You have 100 SOL staked. Unstake 30 SOL, keep 70 SOL earning rewards.

### How it works

1. Enter an amount less than your total staked balance
2. Choose Instant or Slow mode
3. Hubra splits your stake account automatically
4. The unstaked portion processes, the rest keeps earning

### Fees

| Fee | Amount | Notes |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Account rent | ~0.002 SOL | One-time fee for creating split account |
| Sanctum fee | 0.1-0.5% | Only if using Instant mode |
| Network fees | Covered | Hubra pays (gasless) |

<Info>
Minimum unstake amount: 0.01 SOL
</Info>

## Which should I choose?

| Situation | Recommended |
|-----------|-------------|
| Need SOL urgently | Instant |
| Market opportunity | Instant |
| Can wait 2-3 days | Slow |
| Large amount (>1000 SOL) | Slow |
| Unstaking small portion | Either works |

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why is Instant unstake output less than Slow?">
    Instant unstake uses Sanctum's liquidity pools to swap your stake for SOL immediately. This convenience comes with a small fee (0.1-0.5%). Slow unstake uses native Solana deactivation with no intermediary fees.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How long does Slow unstake take?">
    2-3 days. Solana requires stake to be deactivated for one full epoch before withdrawal. Epoch duration is approximately 2-3 days.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel an unstake?">
    Instant: No, it's immediate. Slow: Yes, you can re-delegate before the cooldown completes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum unstake amount?">
    Yes, 0.01 SOL minimum. For partial unstakes, the remaining balance must cover rent exemption (~0.002 SOL).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
