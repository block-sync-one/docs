## How instant unstake works

Convert your staked SOL to liquid SOL immediately. Powered by [Sanctum](https://sanctum.so).

**Steps:**
1. Select your stake account
2. Choose full or partial unstake
3. See the output amount and fees
4. Confirm the transaction
5. SOL arrives in your wallet within seconds

<Info>
Hubra covers all network fees. You only pay the Sanctum liquidity fee.
</Info>

### Fees

| Fee | Amount | Who pays |
|-----|--------|----------|
| Sanctum fee | 0.1-0.5% | You |
| Network fee | ~0.000005 SOL | Hubra (gasless) |

The exact Sanctum fee depends on available liquidity. You'll see the fee before confirming.

<Warning>
If price impact exceeds 5%, you'll see a warning. Consider unstaking in smaller portions for large amounts.
</Warning>

## Partial unstake

Unstake any amount - you don't have to unstake everything at once.

**Example:** You have 100 SOL staked. Unstake 30 SOL instantly, keep 70 SOL earning rewards.

### How it works

1. Enter an amount less than your total staked balance
2. Hubra splits your stake account automatically
3. The unstaked portion converts to SOL instantly
4. The remaining stake keeps earning rewards

### Fees

| Fee | Amount | Notes |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Account rent | ~0.002 SOL | One-time fee for creating split account |
| Sanctum fee | 0.1-0.5% | Liquidity fee |
| Network fees | Covered | Hubra pays (gasless) |

<Info>
Minimum unstake amount: 0.01 SOL
</Info>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why is the output slightly less than my staked amount?">
    Instant unstake uses Sanctum's liquidity pools to swap your stake for SOL immediately. This convenience comes with a small fee (0.1-0.5%).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum unstake amount?">
    Yes, 0.01 SOL minimum. For partial unstakes, the remaining balance must cover rent exemption (~0.002 SOL).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I unstake large amounts?">
    Yes, but for very large amounts (1000+ SOL), consider unstaking in portions to minimize price impact from the liquidity pool.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How fast is instant unstake?">
    SOL arrives in your wallet within seconds of confirming the transaction.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
