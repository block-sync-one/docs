## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### How often does Auto Earn run?

Hubra Copilot runs on a regular schedule to continuously monitor and optimize your assets. The exact frequency is optimized to balance efficiency with network costs.

### Can I pause or stop Auto Earn?

Yes! You can pause or stop Auto Earn at any time by revoking the delegation permission in your settings. Your funds will remain in their current positions, but no new optimizations will occur.

### What tokens are supported?

Auto Earn supports all major tokens that have available yield opportunities on integrated protocols. The system automatically detects which tokens in your wallet can earn yield.

### Are there any fees?

Hubra Copilot operates on your behalf using standard blockchain transaction fees. There are no additional Hubra fees for using Auto Earn.

### How do I see what Auto Earn has done?

All Auto Earn activities are recorded in your **activity feed**. You can see:
- When deposits were made
- When migrations occurred
- Which protocols were used
- Current APY for each position

### What happens if I want to withdraw my funds?

You can withdraw your funds at any time. Simply navigate to your portfolio, select the position you want to close, and withdraw. There are no lockup periods or withdrawal restrictions.

### Is my money safe?

Hubra Copilot only uses vetted lending protocols and includes multiple safety checks. However, DeFi participation carries inherent risks (see Risks section above). Your funds are never held by Hubra‚Äîthey remain in the protocols you're earning from.

### Can I customize the strategy?

Currently, Auto Earn uses an optimized default strategy. Customization options may be available in future updates.

### What's the minimum amount needed?

Auto Earn requires tokens worth at least **$5** to make a deposit. This minimum threshold prevents unnecessary micro-transactions and ensures cost-effectiveness.

### How does it compare to manual earning?

Auto Earn offers several advantages over manual earning:
- **24/7 Optimization**: Works continuously, even when you're not checking
- **Time Savings**: No need to research or manually move funds
- **Automatic Migration**: Moves funds to better opportunities automatically
- **Consistent Monitoring**: Never misses an opportunity

However, manual earning gives you more control over specific positions and strategies.
