## A few things to know

### Portfolio intelligence

Ask about your holdings and get more than numbers:

- **Allocation breakdown** showing where your money actually sits
- **Risk flags** for concentration, volatile tokens, and liquidity concerns
- **Performance tracking** across your positions
- **Recent activity** and pending transactions

Horus doesn't just report. It tells you what those numbers mean for your situation.

> "What's my portfolio look like?"

Horus shows holdings, allocation percentages, and flags that you're 60% in one token. That's concentration risk you should know about.

### Token swaps

Execute trades through Jupiter with full transparency:

- Best route selection across all Solana DEXs
- Price impact and fee breakdown before you sign
- Slippage protection built in

> "Swap my USDC to SOL"

Horus pulls your exact balance, routes through Jupiter for best execution, and shows the estimated output before you sign anything.

### Chained operations

String multiple actions together in one message:

> "Swap all my USDC to SOL and then stake it"

Horus chains the transactions. One message, two operations.

### Limit orders

Set your price, walk away:

> "Buy 1 SOL at $50"

Creates an order that fills when the price hits your target.

### DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging)

Split your entry across multiple orders:

> "Put $500 into SOL over 5 daily orders"

Horus sets up the schedule and executes automatically.

### Staking and yield

Put your tokens to work:

- **SOL staking** to raSOL via Sanctum with APY comparison
- **Instant unstake** without waiting periods
- **Earn vaults** filtered by token, risk level, and expected returns
- **Deposits and withdrawals** through conversation

> "Stake my SOL"

Horus shows you options, expected yields, and handles the delegation.

### Perpetual trading

For leverage exposure:

- SOL, BTC, ETH futures up to 100x
- Long and short positions
- Clear risk info showing entry price, liquidation price, and unrealized PnL

> "Open a 5x long on SOL"

Horus shows you the math before you commit.

### Transfers

Send tokens by wallet address or saved contact:

> "Send 5 SOL to mom"

If mom is in your address book, done. If not, Horus asks for the address.

### Price alerts

Never miss a move:

> "Alert me when SOL drops below $100"

Horus checks every 2 minutes. Up to 10 active alerts per wallet.

### Portfolio rebalancing

One message to restructure your holdings:

> "Rebalance to 50% SOL, 25% USDC, 25% raSOL"

Horus calculates the swaps, shows routes and fees, presents a transaction card. You approve, it executes.

## Gasless transactions

All transactions on Hubra are gasless. Hubra covers the network fees.

No more "insufficient balance for gas" errors. 
No more keeping SOL aside just to pay for other transactions.

<Info>
Platform fees are 0.5 to 1% on swaps. Limit orders and DCA positions have a small setup fee.
</Info>

## Horus remembers you

This isn't a stateless chatbot. Horus keeps track of:

- **Your preferences** like risk tolerance, default slippage, favorite tokens
- **Your history** and patterns from previous conversations
- **Your goals** and context that shapes how it responds

The more you use it, the better it gets at understanding what you actually want.

---

## Built to push back

Horus has opinions and shares them.

When recommending something, it shows the math. If you ask to swap $17 of stablecoins for a vault with 0.5% more APY, Horus will tell you the swap fee makes it a bad trade.

A lot of AI assistants just execute whatever you ask. Horus tells you when the numbers don't work.
