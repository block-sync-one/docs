# Horus

Horus is the AI inside Hubra. You talk to it in plain English. It manages your portfolio.

Not a chatbot that gives advice. An agent that executes.

---

## The idea

Most DeFi tools assume you already know what you're doing. Horus doesn't.

You tell it what you want. It figures out the how.

> "Swap half my USDC to SOL and stake it"

One message. Two transactions. Done.

## A few things to know

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="You control everything">
    Horus builds transactions. You sign them. Your keys never leave your wallet.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No gas fees">
    Hubra covers network fees. You don't need SOL set aside for transactions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="It learns you">
    Horus remembers your preferences, risk tolerance, and patterns. Gets better over time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="It pushes back">
    If the math doesn't work, Horus tells you. Won't execute bad trades just because you asked.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Example

**You:** "What do I have?"

**Horus:** Shows your holdings, allocation, and flags that you're 60% in one token.

**You:** "Rebalance to 50% SOL, 30% USDC, 20% raSOL"

**Horus:** Calculates swaps, shows the transaction card. You approve.

**You:** "Alert me if SOL drops 10%"

**Horus:** Done.

Three messages. Full rebalance with monitoring.
