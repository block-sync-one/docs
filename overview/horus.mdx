# Horus — Your AI Financial Copilot

Horus is your way to manage crypto on Solana through conversation. Tell it what you want, it figures out the how.

---

## What is Horus?

Horus lives inside your Hubra wallet. Instead of clicking through menus and hunting for the right button, you just talk to it.

**Example:**
> "What do I have in my wallet?"

Horus shows you what you own, how it's split across tokens, and flags anything that looks off—like 80% of your money sitting in one volatile asset.

---

## What can Horus do?

### Check your portfolio
Ask about your holdings. Horus shows you what you have, what it's worth today, and how your allocation looks.

### Swap tokens
Want to trade?
> "Swap my USDC to SOL"

Horus finds the best route, shows you what you'll get, and waits for your approval.

### Stake and earn
> "Stake my SOL"

Horus picks the best option and shows you expected returns before you commit.

### Set alerts
> "Let me know when SOL hits $150"

Horus checks every few minutes and pings you when it happens. Up to 10 alerts active at once.

### Send tokens
> "Send 10 USDC to Alex"

If Alex is in your contacts, done. If not, Horus asks for the address.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="You type what you want">
    Normal language, no special syntax
  </Step>
  <Step title="Horus figures it out">
    Checks your balance, finds the best route, builds the transaction
  </Step>
  <Step title="You approve">
    Look at the details, sign if it looks right
  </Step>
</Steps>

Nothing moves without your signature.

## A few things to know

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="You approve everything">
    Horus can't move your money without you signing. Every transaction shows you exactly what's happening first.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No gas fees">
    Hubra covers network fees. You don't need to keep SOL around just to pay for transactions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="It learns your preferences">
    The more you chat with Horus, the better it gets at understanding what you want. It remembers your risk tolerance, your favorite tokens, your patterns.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="It tells you when something's a bad idea">
    If the math doesn't work—like swapping $17 for a vault with 0.5% better yield when fees eat more than that—Horus will say so.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Horus a wallet?">
    No. It works with your Hubra wallet. Think of it as the brain, not the vault.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can Horus access my keys?">
    No. Your keys stay secure. Horus builds transactions; you sign them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I mess up?">
    Horus shows you exactly what will happen before you sign. If something looks wrong, don't approve it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I ask Horus about non-crypto stuff?">
    It'll politely redirect you. Horus only does crypto.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
