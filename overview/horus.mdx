# Horus

Horus is your AI-powered DeFi assistant built right into Hubra. Instead of navigating menus and filling out forms, just tell Horus what you want in plain language — and it handles the rest.

Think of Horus as a sharp financial copilot who understands your portfolio, knows the Solana ecosystem, and can actually execute trades on your behalf.

## What Can Horus Do?

### 💬 Chat Naturally

Ask Horus anything about your portfolio, market conditions, or DeFi strategies. No need to learn complex interfaces — just type like you're texting a friend.

**Example prompts:**
- "How's my portfolio doing?"
- "What's the price of SOL?"
- "Should I stake or earn yield on my USDC?"

---

### 📊 Portfolio Insights

Horus sees your full Hubra portfolio and gives you instant analysis:

- **Token balances** and current values
- **Yield positions** — where you're earning and at what APY
- **Staking overview** — your staked SOL and rewards
- **Open orders** — limit orders and DCA schedules
- **Performance tracking** — how your portfolio has changed over time

---

### 🔄 Execute Trades

Tell Horus to swap, stake, earn, or transfer — and it builds the transaction for you. You always review and approve before anything executes.

**What Horus can do:**

| Action | Example |

| **Stake SOL** | "Stake 5 SOL" |

## How It Works

1. **Open Horus** — tap the Horus icon in the Hubra app
2. **Type your request** — or pick from the suggested prompts
3. **Horus analyzes** — it checks your portfolio, fetches market data, and figures out the best approach
4. **Review the plan** — Horus shows you exactly what it wants to do, with full transaction details
5. **Approve & execute** — tap to confirm, and the transaction goes through

<Frame>
  The entire flow happens in a single chat conversation. No switching between pages.
</Frame>

---

## The Approval Flow

When Horus prepares a transaction, you'll see a **transaction card** with:

- **What's happening** — e.g., "Swap 10 SOL → USDC"
- **Estimated output** — how much you'll receive
- **Route details** — which protocol or DEX is used
- **Fee breakdown** — network fees and any platform fees

You can **approve**, **modify**, or **reject** any transaction. Horus never auto-executes without your say-so.

## FAQ

**Is Horus free?**
Every user gets 20 free credits. After that, credits are part of the Hubra platform.

**Does Horus have access to my private keys?**
No. Horus prepares transactions, but signing happens on your device. Your keys never leave your wallet.

**Can Horus make trades without my approval?**
No. Every transaction requires your explicit approval before it executes.

**What AI model powers Horus?**
Horus is powered by state-of-the-art language models, optimized for DeFi understanding and Solana-specific knowledge.

**Can I see my chat history?**
Yes. Your conversation history is saved and accessible from the Horus interface.

**What tokens does Horus support?**
All tokens available on Hubra — including SOL, USDC, and thousands of SPL tokens.
